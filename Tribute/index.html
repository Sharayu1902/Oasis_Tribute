About the Lata Mangeshkar

Lata Mangeshkar was an Indian singer.
She was born on 28 September 1929 and passed away on 6 February 2022.
The Indian playback singer was considered to be one of the greatest singers of all time.
She made a huge contribution to the Indian music industry.
Her singing career spans over a decade.
She gained many honorific titles during her career.
She is often called the Nightingale of India and the Voice of the Millennium
